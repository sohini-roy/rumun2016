About Us

Ravenshaw University Model United Nations 2016 promises to be an enterprising event that proves to be a platform for simulating debate and discussion. Our conference is a platform for young adults to develop their debating prowess, logical reasoning, diplomacy and social skills. It delves deep into several issues which plague humanity, and determinedly strives to come up with innovative solutions.

The Third Edition of Ravenshaw University Model United Nations is going to be organized in collaboration with the United Nations Information Centre for India and Bhutan which in itself is a testimony of the grandeur of the conference. The prospect of dignitaries like the Governor of the State, His Excellency, Dr. S.C Jamir, Director, United Nations Information Centre for India being the few notable ones amongst the congregation of eminent personalities eager to grace the conference with their presence exalts the amplitude of the quality of our conference.

Ravenshaw University Model United Nations 2016 shall transpire from within the hallowed halls of the Seven Pillars of Wisdom of Ravenshaw University which shelters in its grand structure the modest chronicles of Ravenshaw’s past glory and the possibilities of its radiant future.

Hope to see you at this year’s Ravenshaw University Model UN Conference.

Committees

Agenda :

1. Reviewing the United Nations Charter.

Strength: 100 Participating Countries

Level: Advance

The United Nations Charter Review Conference 2016 is a trademark brainchild simulation at Ravenshaw University Model United Nations 2016. Bearing in the mind the 70 long years of ramification and churning that the international geopolitical dynamics has undergone and with a new array of complex kaleidoscopic problems plaguing the humanity to its very edge, the world needs a more reformed and transparent United Nations to redressal the grievances of its Seven Billion Humans.
Under the provisions laid down in the Chapter XVIII of the United Nations Charter, the Office of the Secretary-General has called for a Conference to review the United Nations Charter at Ravenshaw University Model United Nation 2016 this July with the unanimous support from the General Assembly and the Security Council.

Agenda :

1. Sovereignty vs. Humanitarian Duty : Discussing the Legality of Humanitarian Interventions and Conflicts.

Strength: 120 Participating Countries

Level: Intermediate

The United Nations Secretary-General Ban Ki-moon has called for a global response to address the suffering of millions of women, men and children affected by humanitarian crisis. The first-ever World Humanitarian Summit is our opportunity to confront these global challenges head-on and generate greater global leadership and political will to end the conflict, alleviate suffering and reduce risk.
This year, Istanbul is going to witness the largest conglomeration of the world's most powerful and the world most oppressed in a backdrop of major Sectarian conflicts, Refugee crisis and Economic meltdown, negotiating to forge a declaration to restore every human's faith in United Nation's lofty idea of " One World, One family." Something which we intend to replicate at this Brainchild Simulation of WHS at RUMUN 2016.

Agenda :

1. Towards Gender Equality: Elimination of all forms of Violence towards Women while countering negative gender roles and stereotypes.

Strength: 100 Participating Countries

Level: Beginner

UN Women, the United Nations Entity for Gender Equality and the Empowerment of Women was a historic step by the Member States towards accelerating goals on gender equality and the empowerment of women. The main roles of UN Women are to support inter-governmental bodies, such as the Commission on the Status of Women, in their formulation of policies, global standards and norms, to help Member States to implement these standards, often by providing suitable technical and financial support and to lead and coordinate the UN system’s work on gender equality as well as promote accountability, including through regular monitoring of system-wide progress.
United Nations Women is a dynamic and strong champion for women and girls, providing them with a powerful voice at the global, regional and local levels.

Agenda :

1. Analysing the significance of "sedition” law in current Indian Scenario.
2. Analysing “Section 377of the Indian Penal Code” subsequent to latest developments.

Strength: 80 Political Party Representatives

 Level: Intermediate

The All India Political Parties Meet is a replication of UN Council consisting of prominent representatives from around the country which assemble to discuss, debate and deliberate upon some of the greatest issues that the country faces today. Unlike the Lok Sabha or other Parliamentary bodies, AIPPM is not a binding committee and hence the resolutions passed here cannot be perceived in the form of laws or schemes.Some issues are just too broad and critical to be efficiently addressed in the often hectic Lok Sabha agendas. So, a committee such as AIPPM provides the representatives with a platform to address one such particular issue at a time, comprehensively and efficiently, find probable solutions and follow them up in the legislative bodies.
The aim of this scrupulous committee is to come up with a guideline based elucidation after ample discussions between the representative of political parties and personalities. The solution shall take the form of an outcome (communiqué) and it shall be passed with a majority. World Humanitarian Summit 2016
